About DSC

DSC VIT powered by Google Developers is a student community at VIT Vellore. We strive to bring about technological innovations among students and we do so by providing a platform for them to exhibit their talents. We are a group of passionate designers, developers and managers who work together to bring about collaborative results which better the lives of everyone around us.

About VIT Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore VIT, Vellore is one of the top-rated engineering colleges in India. Diversity is the strongest aspect of the institute and the students from various states and nations, the key to its reputation. The university works on ideas and executes tasks of different domains under Clubs and Chapters.

Hackathon

The 24-hour hackathon aims to bring out the best developers to put their skills and creativity against the clock to come up with a solution for a real-world problem. There is a minimum requirement of 50% female participation in each team, to ensure and promote women’s participation in tech and tech-related events.
Don’t know how to code? Or Don’t know how to implement a certain idea? No worries, our experts will be there every step of the way to mentor and guide you as you build your product, your participation is what that matters

Our Vision We have been organising Women Techies for the past 2 years with a goal to encourage and facilitate the participation of women in the tech space. Succeeding in tech is no easy task, but, by providing women with opportunities and platforms to exhibit their skills, we hope to put them right at the top of the leaderboard. Knowing that our efforts benefit so many talented women and being able to see the satisfaction on our participant’s faces makes all of us extremely proud in the work that we do.
